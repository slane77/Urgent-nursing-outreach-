Candidate Engine — staff guide

One page on how to sign in, work your candidates, and get files audit-ready. Five minutes to read.

1 · Sign in

1
Go to urgent-nursing-outreach.vercel.app/candidates.html and sign in with your Day Webster email + password (same login across all pages). First time in? Ask an admin for your temporary password, then use Change my password on the Admin page.
2
You'll see candidates on your desks only. If your list is empty, an admin needs to tick your desks on Admin → People & desks. Admins see everything.

2 · How a candidate flows through

1
They register (web form or chat). The system captures their details, profession, PIN, address and consent — and auto-routes them to the right desk. Their document checklist opens and they're emailed a secure upload link automatically.
2
They upload documents via their personal portal link (or email). Each document is pre-checked by AI against our compliance rules — clear problems (expired, wrong type, blurry) are automatically bounced back to the candidate with a friendly fix-it email. Sensitive items are never auto-emailed; they wait for you.
3
You review. Everything arrives as unverified — for audit. Open the candidate, look at each document (View document), read the AI pre-check, and mark Verified or Unsuitable (with a reason).
4
Full file audit → sign-off. When every requirement is verified, click Sign off → Maintenance. That records you + the date, and marks the candidate compliant and bookable. If a document later expires, they drop to Requires update automatically and get chased daily.

3 · The buttons on a candidate's file

ButtonWhat it does
Run agentThe AI recruiter engages/qualifies the candidate and emails them (never makes compliance decisions).
Email document request(Re)sends their personalised checklist + secure upload link from compliance@.
Copy portal linkCopies their personal upload link — paste into a text or WhatsApp.
View document / Run AI reviewOpens the stored file securely / re-runs the AI pre-check on it.
Sign off → MaintenanceThe audit gate: candidate becomes compliant & bookable. Auditors only.
Requires updateDrops a lapsed candidate out of compliance until re-audited.
Audit packPrintable per-candidate compliance file for framework audits.

The golden rule: the system collects, checks and chases — but nothing counts as verified, and nobody is bookable, until a human auditor has reviewed the file and signed it off to Maintenance. AI pre-checks help you work faster; they are never the decision.
