The Candidate Engine

An AI-assisted system to attract, qualify and compliance-ready candidates across every Day Webster vertical.

The goal

Build Day Webster's own recruiter engine: a candidate finds us (or we find them), an AI agent engages, qualifies and begins the compliance process automatically — and a human steps in for every judgement call. One tidy bench across nursing, doctors, AHP, complex care, care homes, children's services and insurance, that becomes our system of record and later syncs into Eclipse.

The principle throughout: automate the collecting and chasing; humans make the decisions. The agent can request documents and references — it never decides if they're acceptable or marks anyone work-ready.

How compliance works

The system gathers and chases; our auditors verify and sign off. Nothing the AI collects is treated as verified until a person audits the full file.

  • ProcessingThe candidate is sending documents in. The AI checks each against our parameters, re-asks for anything wrong or unclear, and builds the file — all held unverified, for audit.
  • MaintenanceAuditors have reviewed every aspect of the file and signed it off — the candidate is compliant and bookable to work (recorded with who signed off and when).
  • Requires updateA maintained candidate has fallen out of compliance (e.g. a document expired). They're flagged and chased back through to re-audit.
