Add "Register your interest" to the Day Webster website

Copy one of the snippets below into the website (CMS / page HTML block). Both point at the live registration form, which routes candidates straight to the right desk.

Option 1 — a button (recommended)

Drops a green button anywhere. Simplest and works everywhere.

Register your interest
<a href="https://urgent-nursing-outreach.vercel.app/intake.html"
   style="display:inline-block;background:#16a34a;color:#fff;
          font:600 16px system-ui,sans-serif;padding:14px 28px;
          border-radius:8px;text-decoration:none">
  Register your interest
</a>

Option 2 — embed the whole form in a page

Shows the form inline on a "Join us / Candidates" page.

<iframe src="https://urgent-nursing-outreach.vercel.app/intake.html"
   title="Register with Day Webster"
   style="width:100%;max-width:600px;height:1000px;border:0"></iframe>

Option 3 — a plain link

For email signatures, social posts, job adverts.

https://urgent-nursing-outreach.vercel.app/intake.html

Printable QR poster

For job fairs, wards, noticeboards: open register-poster.html and tap "Print / Save as PDF".
